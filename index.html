How this estimate works

Initial capital retained equals the difference between the two equipment packages. Operating profit is modeled as rooms in service × revenue per room × margin, with the selected annual growth rate. Retirement value compounds retained capital until retirement and compounds the selected share of each year’s incremental profit for the remaining years.
